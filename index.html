Plot

Years after witnessing the murder of his parents, billionaire playboy Bruce Wayne travels around the world to be taken in by the League of Shadows to learn how to fight evil. With the plan to rid his home, Gotham City,of the corrupt he adopt the image of his childhood fears to become Batman.
